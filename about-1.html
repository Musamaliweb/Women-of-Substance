Who We Are

A women-led movement for empowerment and global engagement

Queens of Substance is a dynamic community-based organization dedicated to empowering women, uplifting communities, and creating opportunities for sustainable socio-economic growth.

We focus on mentorship, entrepreneurship, and capacity building, equipping women and youth with the tools they need to thrive. Through collaboration and unity, we create platforms where ideas are shared, opportunities are unlocked, and lives are transformed.

Our work extends beyond local impact into global spaces, where we actively participate in international forums and conferences, representing our community and contributing to conversations that shape the future.
