FAQs

All Your Answers in One Place

Who are the Queens of Substance?

Queens of Substance is a movement and platform dedicated to empowering women to embrace their worth, confidence, and purpose. We create safe spaces, impactful content, and opportunities for growth, connection, and transformation.

Who is this community for?

This community is for women who are ready to grow — emotionally, mentally, spiritually, and financially. Whether you're rediscovering yourself or leveling up, you belong here.

How can I get involved?

You can join our events, engage with our content, volunteer, or partner with us. We’re always open to women who want to contribute, collaborate, or simply be part of something meaningful.

Do you offer mentorship or programs?

Yes. We provide mentorship, empowerment programs, and curated sessions designed to help women build confidence, develop skills, and unlock opportunities.

Is there a membership fee?

Some of our programs and events may have a fee, but many of our resources and community engagements are accessible. Our goal is to keep impact inclusive while sustaining growth.

How can I partner or collaborate?

We welcome partnerships with brands, organizations, and individuals aligned with our mission. Reach out through our contact form and let’s build something impactful together.
